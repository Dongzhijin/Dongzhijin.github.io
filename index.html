Profile

I am passionate about advancing AGI and solving real-world problems using AI technologies.

Research Interests

  • Post-Training of Large Language Models (LLMs) or Multi-modal Large Models
  • Focused on building LLM agents with capabilities in CoT reasoning, tool use, long-term memory, and self-correction for robust decision-making in complex environments.

Featured Projects

  • Not All Preferences are What You Need for Post-Training: Designed a selective alignment strategy to optimize user preferences during post-training.
  • Post-Training of Large Models with Preference Optimization: Developed reward model evaluation frameworks and efficient online DPO pipelines.
  • Multi-Normal Prototypes Learning for Weakly Supervised Anomaly Detection: Proposed frameworks for anomaly detection with limited labeled data.
